## 💡 Writing Tips

### Do's:
✅ Be specific about technologies and concepts
✅ Quantify impact when possible (metrics, user counts)
✅ Use action verbs (Built, Designed, Optimized, Implemented)
✅ Show your thinking process (challenges and solutions)
✅ Keep it concise but informative
✅ Proofread for typos and grammar

### Don'ts:
❌ Don't use vague terms ("worked on", "helped with")
❌ Don't overstate your skills or experience
❌ Don't use jargon without context
❌ Don't make it too long (recruiters scan quickly)
❌ Don't forget to highlight backend work
